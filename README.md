SemaNami — Digital Health & Emergency Command Center
SemaNami ni mfumo jumuishi wa afya wa kidijitali (Enterprise Health Platform) ulioundwa nchini Tanzania. Siyo tu tovuti, bali ni "Command Center" inayounganisha wagonjwa, madaktari, na huduma za dharura kupitia teknolojia ya kisasa ya AI (Artificial Intelligence), Blockchain, na GPRS Monitoring.
 Live Site: semanamimi.com
Architecture: PHP 8+, MySQL, Larevel,Tailwind CSS, JavaScript (ES6+), Node.js (AI Node).
 Sifa Kuu za Kimapinduzi (Core Innovations)
 1. AI Symptom Intelligence & Smart Routing
Tofauti na mifumo mingine, SemaNami haitoi maelezo tu, bali inatenda:
Decision Tree Logic: AI inachakata dalili (Symptom Checking) na kufanya Probability Scoring.
Smart Routing: Ikitambua dalili za ujauzito, inakuunganisha na Mkunga/Nurse; ikitambua homoni, inakuunganisha na Endocrinologist.
Hormonal Guard (7-25 yrs): Bot maalum inayofuatilia ukuaji na mabadiliko ya homoni kwa wasichana na kutoa tahadhari (Alerts) mapema yakionekana mabadiliko hatarishi.
2. Emergency Trigger & GPRS Localization
GPRS Tracking: Mgonjwa anapobonyeza SOS, Admin anaona ramani ya dharura Live.
Vitochi Bridge: Inatumia Cell Tower Triangulation kuwapata watu wasio na smartphones.
112 Integration: API maalum iliyo tayari kuunganishwa na namba za dharura za serikali (Police/Ambulance).
 3. Blockchain Ledger & Self-Destruct
Data Integrity: Kila huduma inayotolewa inarekodiwa kwenye Private Ledger kwa kutumia Blockchain Technology kuzuia rekodi kubadilishwa na daktari au admin.
90-Days Self-Destruct: Chat za siri (Private symptoms) zinafutwa kiotomatiki baada ya siku 9 kumlinda mgonjwa, huku rekodi rasmi (Medical Records) zikibaki encrypted.

 4. Voice AI & SMS Health Tips
Voice AI: Inatumia Speech-to-Text ya Kiswahili kuelewa wagonjwa wanaopiga simu za sauti na kuwapa maelekezo ya kwanza.
SMS Health Tips: Inatuma ushauri wa afya na homoni kwa watumiaji wa vitochi kulingana na umri na historia yao.

 Tofauti ya SemaNami na Mifumo Mingine (The Edge)
Localization: Imejengwa kwa ajili ya Watanzania (Kiswahili, USSD, Vitochi Support).
Proactive AI: Mifumo mingine inasubiri uulize, SemaNami AI inakupa Reminder ya dawa na ushauri wa daktari (Post-Consultation Care).
HMIS Sync: Inauwezo wa kuunganishwa (Interoperability) na mifumo ya hospitali zilizopo ili kubadilishana data za wagonjwa kwa usalama.
